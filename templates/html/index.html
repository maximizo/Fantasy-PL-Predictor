Premier League Fantasy Soccer

Luis Bejaran, Megan Cogguillo, Julissa Guzman, and Max Izotov

Our Project

Our project utilizes Premier League Fantasy soccer data to help participants select their ultimate Fantasy team. We leverage both historical player data ('17 - '20) from the league as well as points data from the current Fantasy season. Our project uses Tableau to produce interactive dashboards that help the user assess how to spend their limited budget as well as easily access key player metrics from past seasons. We also built a machine learning model that use points and performance data from the current Fantasy season to analyze trends and catch anomalies

Fantasy Soccer 101

Each week, participants select a team composed of 10 players (constrained by a budget of $100m) and receive points based on the playersâ€™ actual performance that week. Points are given based on goals scored, goals assisted, play time, penalty saves, and other metrics; points are also deducted based on player misconduct (red and yellow cards), penalty misses, own goals, and goal concessions. The full scoring rules are available here.

Scoring Rules

Action Points
For playing up to 60 minutes 1
For playing 60 minutes or more (excluding stoppage time) 2
For each goal scored by a goalkeeper or defender 6
For each goal scored by a midfielder 5
For each goal scored by a forwards 4
For each goal assist 3
For a clean sheet by a goalkeeper or defender 4
For a clean sheet by a midfielder 1
For every 3 shot saves by a goalkeeper 1
For each penalty save 5
For each penalty miss -2
Bonus points for the best players in a match 1-3
For every 2 goals conceded by a goalkeeper or defender -1
For each yellow card -1
For red yellow card -3
For each own goal -2
